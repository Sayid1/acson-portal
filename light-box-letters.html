Quality Light Box Letters for Businesses

Our internally illuminated light boxes with digitally printed translucent graphics and opal acrylic or polycarbonate faces, are an extremely flexible option that are custom built to your specifications. The exact material depends on several factors. Do you need a clear window for posters, or are you looking to incorporate a large logo and lettering into your design? If you are adding push-thru letters, acrylic is typically the material used for the face. Colours for both the metal siding and faces can be fully customized.

The sides of light boxes are typically made from aluminum. This material is highly resistant to corrosion and temperature fluctuations. In a single-faced sign, the rear can also be fabricated using aluminum.

DOMO can supply light boxes in all formats, from slimline edge lit for retail display to double-sided under verandah light boxes, wall-mounted light boxes, and flexiface illuminated billboards.

LED Display Menu Board

LED display menu board is designed specifically for fast food restaurants, bars, and cafes, making it an excellent choice for attracting customer attention. Featuring high-brightness LED lights, menu board ensures clear visibility of content even in brightly lit environments. The easy-to-use wireless remote control or mobile app allows for quick and simple updates to menu items, pricing, and promotions, enhancing operational efficiency. Built with durable, waterproof materials, this display is perfect for both indoor and outdoor use. Whether showcasing daily specials, drink recommendations, or limited-time offers, this LED display menu board brings a modern and professional aesthetic to any establishment, significantly improving the customer experience and engagement.

WHY CHOOSE DOMO SIGNS

DOMO Signs is one of the most innovative and professional channel letter sign manufacturing companies in China.

  • Price Advantage

    We are located in Dongguan, China, which is the world's manufacturing system, with a large number of labor resources and relatively low labor costs. This means that products manufactured in us can often hit your budget targets at a more competitive price.

  • High Quality

    All signs have UL, RoHS, and CE certifications and are made with the highest quality materials and proven components. Thanks to the large market and manufacturing scale in China, large-scale production reduces unit costs, allowing us to guarantee high quality at a low price.

  • Durability

    Our signs are typically made of stainless steel, a material that is stronger and more durable than aluminum, stainless steel signs are the most durable. They are built to withstand the harshest environments and are rust resistant, making them a long-lasting investment.

  • Production Capacity and Speed

    Our manufacturing capacity is robust, capable of bulk production in shorter timeframes. This could be advantageous if you require a large quantity of signs within a short period.
